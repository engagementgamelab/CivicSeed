An important part of looking inward is awareness of your social identities in areas such as culture, race, socioeconomic background, spiritual beliefs, and gender. In community-based work, it is essential to not only understand yourself, but where differences and similarities exist between you and the community. Understanding yourself is the primary focus of the first part of the enigma.

How will these factors play a role in your work? How can you work successfully across differences?
